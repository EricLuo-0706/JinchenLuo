- **Spiking Neural Network comparison Research:** [Advancements in Image Classification: Comparing Spiking, Convolutional, and Artificial Neural Networks](https://nhsjs.com/wp-content/uploads/2024/07/Advancements-in-Image-Classification-Comparing-Spiking-Convolutional-and-Artificial-Neural-Network.pdf)
  - Authored an extensive academic research paper exploring the capabilities of Spiking Neural Networks (SNNs) by comparing their image classification performance against traditional Artificial Neural Networks (ANNs) and Convolutional Neural Networks (CNNs) using both static and neuromorphic event-driven datasets.
  - Designed and conducted three experiments on standard commercial hardware to see if the theoretical power efficiencies of SNNs hold up outside of specialized neuromorphic chips.
